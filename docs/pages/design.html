Design Philosophy

The Market Framework design philosophy centers around creating flexible, scalable, and interoperable systems that can adapt to evolving market requirements.

Key Principles

  • Modularity: Components should be loosely coupled and highly cohesive
  • Extensibility: Systems should be designed for future growth and change
  • Interoperability: Standards-based interfaces for seamless integration
  • Security: Security by design with comprehensive threat modeling
  • Usability: User-centered design with clear, intuitive interfaces

Architecture Patterns

Our design framework incorporates several proven architectural patterns:

Event-Driven Architecture

Market events drive system behavior, enabling real-time responsiveness and loose coupling between components.

Microservices Pattern

Decomposing complex market systems into focused, independently deployable services.

API-First Design

Designing APIs as the primary interface, ensuring consistency and enabling ecosystem development.

Market Framework

Collaborative development of market frameworks and regulatory rules.
