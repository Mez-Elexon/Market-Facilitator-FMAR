Community Guidelines

Be Respectful

Treat all participants with respect and professionalism. Constructive criticism is welcome, but personal attacks are not.

Be Constructive

When providing feedback, be specific and constructive. Explain your reasoning and suggest alternatives when possible.

Be Collaborative

Work together to find solutions that benefit the entire industry. Consider the broader impact of your suggestions.

Be Informed

Review relevant documents and discussions before contributing. This helps ensure your input is relevant and valuable.
