Working Group Overview

Our working groups bring together industry stakeholders to collaboratively develop GB flexibility markets. Each group focuses on specific areas and produces materials that inform our development work.

FMAR Design Working Groups

Focused on developing the Flexibility Market Asset Registration solution design.

  • Frequency: Workshop Series (Quarterly)
  • Current Phase: Workshop Series #2
  • Focus Areas: Ecosystem, Data, Application Architecture
  • Participants: Industry stakeholders, technical experts

Rules Development Working Groups

Developing regulatory framework and operational rules for GB flexibility markets.

  • Frequency: Monthly
  • Current Phase: Active Development
  • Focus Areas: Dispatch, E2E Process, Prequal, Agreements
  • Participants: Market participants, regulators, technical experts
