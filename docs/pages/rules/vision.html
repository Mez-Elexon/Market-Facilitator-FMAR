Monthly Working Groups

Vision for Coordination

The Flexibility Market Rules (FMR) development programme aims to create a comprehensive regulatory framework that enables efficient, transparent, and fair operation of GB flexibility markets. Our vision is to establish rules that support market innovation while ensuring system security and consumer protection.

Key Objectives

  • Market Efficiency: Enable efficient dispatch and settlement of flexibility services
  • Transparency: Ensure clear, accessible rules for all market participants
  • Innovation: Support new technologies and business models
  • System Security: Maintain grid stability and reliability
  • Consumer Protection: Safeguard consumer interests and data

Terms of Reference

The Terms of Reference for the Flexibility Market Rules Development programme define the scope, objectives, and governance structure for this work.

Programme Scope

  • Development of dispatch rules and procedures
  • End-to-end process design and optimization
  • Prequalification requirements and processes
  • Standard agreement templates and terms
  • Coordination mechanisms between market participants

Governance Structure

  • Steering Committee: Strategic oversight and decision-making
  • Working Groups: Technical development and stakeholder engagement
  • Expert Panels: Specialized input on specific areas
  • Industry Consultation: Broader stakeholder feedback
